Same Jesus podcast, Season 2

Don’t miss the second season of the Same Jesus podcast, which will address timely topics such as politics, success, suffering, sexuality, holiness, the future of the church and spiritual formation.

  2. Leader Resources
  3. Same Jesus podcast, Season 2
June 17, 2024 / Leader Resources

Russell Joyce and A.J. Swoboda are back, hosting the second season of the Same Jesus podcast.

Russell Joyce is senior pastor of Faith Center, a Foursquare church located in Eugene, Ore. He is also the director of Foursquare Multiply. A.J. Swoboda, Ph.D., is assistant professor of Bible, Theology and World Christianity at Bushnell University. He has written several books, and he planted and pastored a Foursquare church in Portland, Ore. Together, they host the Same Jesus podcast. You can watch the first season here.

This season, Russell and A.J. are joined by new guests every week, including Jordan and Jessica Rice, Rich Villodas, John Mark Comer and many more. Together, they will be delving into timely topics such as politics, success, suffering, sexuality, holiness, the future of the church and spiritual formation. This season will include seven new episodes to better resource and equip you to lead your community with wisdom and grace.
