The Foursquare movement is uniquely positioned to serve the world in word and action with the message of Jesus, the one who brings healing, hope and salvation. We are committed to raising up leaders as we move into the future that Jesus has for us.

Why Foursquare?

What makes us a movement worth joining? Foursquare offers a biblical foundation with the flexibility your church needs to reach its community for Jesus in the modern era.

We are on mission

We began with global missions and evangelism. We are motivated by the Great Commission to make disciples, from our own neighborhoods to remote regions where the gospel has yet to be shared. We empower every believer to expand God's kingdom.

Learn More About Missions

We are in motion

Ministry isn't cookie cutter. As a movement, we recognize things change: society, culture, contexts. And we need to adapt, as well. Never being stagnant, Foursquare is committed to active, relational ministry. We're ready to go wherever God leads us.

Get Involved

We have a message

While our approach is adaptable, our message is sacred. Jesus is the same yesterday, today and forever. His gospel never changes; its goodness never diminishes. We may go many places. We may not all look the same. But our message stays true to God's Word.
